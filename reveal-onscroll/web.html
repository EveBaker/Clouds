What We Do

Discover

We send our agents from city to city, no matter where it is, and locate strange buildings that go against the norm.

Research

Once a building is discovered, we get in contact with the owners and pay them a wealthy amount for us to examine said building. We acquire the layout plans as well.

Implement

If we decide that a building is worthy of our museum, we further compensate the owners and implement it.
